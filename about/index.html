The longer version

I was born in France and raised in Virginia with my twin sister. In 2016, I moved to California to attend Stanford University. There, I was introduced to computer science and UX/UI design. I was instantly drawn to the unique intersection of technology, human psychology, storytelling, and art.

Over the years, I’ve discovered a deep love for the design of complex and crucial technologies. My background in computer science and software engineering has given me invaluable insights about how to be a better designer. My ability to think creatively and critically, and to communicate empathetically and technically, are what set me apart.

I see design as a neverending journey to transform things that don’t make sense into things that do.
