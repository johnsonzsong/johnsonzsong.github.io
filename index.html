PROJECTS

bitesize (2019)
For CS247: Human-Computer Interaction Design Studio, I designed an iOS app that connects Stanford students to individualized and convenient service opportunities at the Haas Center. After multiple rounds of interviews with students, Haas Center advisors, and community service organizations, user personas, and visual design inspiration, this 10-week project resulted in a prototype of a streamlined way for students to help their communities. planit (2018)
For CS147: User Experience Design, I developed an Android app that gamifies the travel-planning process. Through user needfinding, experience prototyping, med-fi prototyping using Figma, and programming the hi-fi prototype in Android Studio, this 10-week project culminated in an app that was demo-ed and pitched in front of students, faculty, and industry professionals at the Hasso Plattner Institute of Design.
